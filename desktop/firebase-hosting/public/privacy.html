Privacy Policy

LyriX Cast (Desktop) & LyriX Remote (Mobile)

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

Faith Companion Studios ("we", "us", or "our") operates the LyriX Cast desktop application and the LyriX Remote mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our applications are designed as completely offline-first, local-network tools. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, sensitive user information, or usage statistics.

  • No Accounts: You do not need to register an account to use either application.
  • No Cloud Storage: All song lyrics, presentation schedules, settings, and databases are stored locally on your device.
  • No Analytics: We do not track how you use the app.

2. Device Permissions Used

To enable the application functionality, we use specific device permissions locally on your Wi-Fi network:

  • Local Network Access (Wi-Fi): LyriX Remote connects directly to LyriX Cast running on your computer over your local Wi-Fi. This network traffic is strictly local to your router and never travels over the internet.
  • Storage Permissions: Used solely to read and write your presentation schedules, databases, and custom lyrics directly on your local device storage.

3. Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with third-party tracking, advertising, or data collection services.

4. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal data, our applications are completely safe for children and comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) regulations.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
